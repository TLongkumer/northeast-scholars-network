About NSN

Non‑profit | Scholar‑led

Our Mission

To cultivate a vibrant network of scholars from and working in the Northeast by enabling mentorship, collaborative learning, and public engagement. We champion rigorous research, ethical practice, and inclusive knowledge production.

  • ✓ Cross‑disciplinary mentorship & peer support
  • ✓ Research circles and writing groups
  • ✓ Workshops on methods, ethics, and publishing
  • ✓ Grants, calls, and opportunity curation

Who We Serve

Undergraduate & master’s students, early PhD scholars, independent researchers, and faculty allies. Whether you’re just starting out or seeking collaborators for advanced projects, you’ll find a community here.

  • ✓ Student researchers building foundational skills
  • ✓ Early‑career academics seeking mentorship
  • ✓ Senior scholars mentoring and co‑authoring

Programs & Activities

Signature offerings that combine rigor with care. Each program is designed to foster skills, confidence, and cross‑institutional connections.

Mentorship Circles

Small cohorts guided by faculty and advanced researchers. Quarterly themes: research design, methods, analysis, and publishing.

Research & Writing Labs

Hands‑on sessions for proposal drafting, IRB/ethics, data analysis, and journal submission pipelines.

Talks & Public Conversations

Author meet‑ups, thematic dialogues, and public scholarship via podcasts and webinars.

Opportunities Desk

Curated fellowships, calls for papers, grants, and summer schools relevant to the Northeast and allied fields.

Community Fieldnotes

A collaborative blog/newsletter featuring field reflections, book notes, and methodological insights.

Skill Workshops

Communication, public speaking, and AI‑augmented research workflows anchored in academic integrity.
