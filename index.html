About NSN

Building bridges in academia through peer support and collaborative learning

The Northeast Scholars Network (NSN) is a community-driven collective for scholars in the social sciences and humanities, hailing from Northeast India and across the country. It was born from the understanding that the academic journey, while rewarding, can also be isolating.

This is not a top-down mentorship program but a circle of peers. The group comprises individuals who have faced, and are still facing, academic challenges and are committed to supporting others on the same path.

Our mission is to create an inclusive space where we can all come together to share our work, exchange ideas, and find support at every stage of our academic journey.

Our Objectives

Guided by a spirit of shared learning and reciprocity

Foster Connection & Collaboration

Create opportunities to connect with peers and mentors across disciplines, encouraging an environment where collaborative research projects can organically emerge.

Promote Open Dialogue

Encourage discussions on research topics, methodologies, and findings to inspire fresh perspectives and provide a platform for sharing work and exploring new ideas.

Offer Peer-to-Peer Support

Share guidance and resources for the research lifecycle, from developing research proposals to navigating publications, particularly for early-career researchers.

Amplify Research & Voices

Provide a platform to present research focused on Northeast India and create opportunities to engage in public scholarship and policy-relevant discussions.

Our Events & Discussions

Interactive sessions designed to foster collaborative learning

Proposal & Writing Workshops

Peer-led sessions where we collectively deconstruct research proposals, thesis chapters, or manuscripts to understand what makes them effective.

Works-in-Progress Sessions

A safe and constructive space to present ongoing research and receive thoughtful feedback from a multidisciplinary group.

Thematic Roundtables

Informal, moderated discussions on specific research themes, methodologies, or challenges designed to spark curiosity and dialogue.

Skill-Sharing Sessions

Members share practical skills they have learned, such as using Generative AI, navigating academic databases, or preparing presentations.

Experience Sharing Circles

Candid conversations where scholars at different stages share their personal journeys, discussing topics like the job market and work-life balance.
